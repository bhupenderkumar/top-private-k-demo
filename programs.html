Academics & Enrichment

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student for success.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program is designed for 3- and 4-year-olds, with a focus on social-emotional learning, literacy, and numeracy skills.

📚

Elementary

Our elementary program serves students in grades K-4, with a focus on building a strong foundation in reading, writing, and mathematics.

🔬

Middle/Upper

Our middle and upper school programs serve students in grades 5-8, with a focus on developing critical thinking, problem-solving, and leadership skills.

🔍

STEM

Our STEM program offers a rigorous and engaging curriculum in science, technology, engineering, and math, with a focus on hands-on learning and real-world applications.

🎨

Arts

Our arts program offers a range of courses in music, theater, dance, and visual arts, with a focus on creativity, self-expression, and collaboration.

🏈

Athletics

Our athletics program offers a range of sports teams for students in grades 5-8, with a focus on teamwork, sportsmanship, and physical fitness.
