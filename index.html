Why Families Choose Us

Top Private K — The Difference

Discover why families across Austin trust us with their children's education.

👩‍🏫

Expert Faculty

Top Private K's experienced and dedicated faculty are passionate about teaching, mentoring, and inspiring our students. Our teachers are certified, trained, and committed to delivering exceptional education.

📚

Small Classes

Our small class sizes ensure personalized attention, support, and instruction for each student. This allows for a deeper understanding of course material, increased confidence, and better academic outcomes.

🏠

Safe Campus

Top Private K prioritizes student safety and well-being. Our campus is secure, maintained, and equipped with modern facilities to support learning, recreation, and community activities.

📊

Curriculum

Our curriculum is designed to cultivate critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills. We incorporate project-based learning, technology integration, and real-world applications to engage students and promote academic excellence.

🧘

Whole-Child Approach

Top Private K's whole-child approach recognizes the unique needs and talents of each student. We foster social-emotional learning, character development, and a growth mindset to help students become confident, compassionate, and capable individuals.

🌈

Community

Our vibrant community is built on mutual respect, trust, and support. Top Private K's students, parents, and faculty work together to create a welcoming, inclusive, and engaging environment that celebrates diversity and promotes academic success.

Our Programs

A comprehensive curriculum that challenges, inspires, and prepares every student.

🎒

Pre-Kindergarten

Our Pre-K program introduces young learners to a love of learning through play-based activities, socialization, and exploration. Our experienced teachers create a nurturing environment that prepares students for future academic success.

📚

Elementary

In our Elementary program, students build foundational skills in reading, writing, math, and social studies. Our dedicated teachers provide individualized support and encourage curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking.

🔬

Middle/Upper

Our Middle and Upper School programs challenge students to think critically, solve problems, and develop a growth mindset. We offer advanced courses, electives, and extracurricular activities that foster academic excellence and personal growth.
