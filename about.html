Our Mission & History

Top Private K has been a cornerstone of Austin's educational landscape since 1995, providing a personalized education to approximately 200 students. Our campus is designed to inspire creativity, curiosity, and a love of learning.

Our teaching approach emphasizes hands-on learning, critical thinking, and character development. We believe in cultivating the whole child – academically, artistically, and athletically.

From STEM programs to arts and athletics, we offer a rich array of extracurricular activities that foster teamwork, leadership, and a sense of community. Our students develop essential life skills, friendships, and a strong sense of purpose.
