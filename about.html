Our Mission & History

Established in 2010, Top Private K has grown from a small group of families seeking a personalized education to a thriving school community of over 200 students. Our campus, nestled in the heart of Austin, provides the perfect balance of natural beauty and urban amenities.

Our curriculum is designed to inspire a love of learning, with a focus on project-based learning, hands-on activities, and STEM education. Our experienced teachers work closely with students to create personalized learning plans that cater to their unique needs and interests.

From arts and music programs to sports teams and clubs, we offer a wide range of extracurricular activities that help our students develop their passions and build meaningful relationships with their peers.
